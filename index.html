About Mariele

Mariele Klering is a Brazilian writer and resilience advocate based in New Zealand. After over a decade in the tech industry, she stepped onto the Jiu-Jitsu mat during a time of personal upheaval—and never left. Her writing explores the intersections of strength, loss, identity, and the quiet power of doing hard things. She is the author of The Mat as a Mirror, a book about how movement can hold, challenge, and reshape us.
